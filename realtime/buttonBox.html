Setup:
  1. Analog In: Attach the wiper of a 10kΩ potentiometer to AIN6 (P9_36). Attach the other ends to VDD_ACD (P9_32) and GNDA_ADC (P9_34).
  2. gpio: Attach a switch between gpio0_7 (P9_42) and GND (P9_1).
  3. i2c: Attach a TMP101 temperature sensor. Pin 1 (SCL) goes to P9_19 (i2c2_scl), Pin 2 (GND) to P9_2 (DGND), Pin 3 (ALERT) no connection, Pin 4 ((SDA) to P9_20 (i2c2_sda), Pin 5 (ADD0) to Pin P9_2 (GND) so address will be 0x48, Pin 6 (V+) to P9_3 (VDD_3V3).
  4. LED Brightnes: Attach an LED in series with a 220Ω resistor between P9_1 (DGND) and P9_14 (ehrpwm1A).
